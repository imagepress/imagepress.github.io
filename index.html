Frequently Asked Questions

Image Press is a web-based tool designed to reduce the file size of images without significant loss of quality. It helps optimize images for faster loading times on websites, saving bandwidth and improving user experience.
The compression process involves resizing and adjusting the quality of the image. When you upload an image, the tool uses canvas and JavaScript to dynamically resize it. The quality is then adjusted to meet a target file size specified by you. The process aims to find an optimal balance between file size reduction and maintaining a visually acceptable image quality.
The Image Press doesn't have a fixed size limit for images. However, the tool's effectiveness depends on the image's original size and content. Larger images may be more challenging to compress to extremely small sizes while preserving quality. It's recommended to experiment with different target file sizes to find the right balance for your specific images.
Image Press is designed to handle various image formats, including popular ones like JPEG, PNG, and others. It supports a wide range of image extensions commonly used on the web. You can upload images with different extensions, and the tool will attempt to compress them accordingly.
No, Image Press does not store the images you upload. The compression process takes place locally in your browser without sending the images to a server. Your privacy and the security of your images are prioritized. Once the compression is complete, you can download the compressed image directly to your device without any data being stored on the server.
